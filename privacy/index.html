Privacy Policy#

Your data isn’t my product.

Deployed doesn’t run ads, use tracking pixels, or load third-party analytics scripts. No cookies. No hidden trackers. Nothing creepy in the background.

The only exception is Cloudflare, which handles security and performance for this site. They may log basic technical info (like IP addresses) as part of their protection services — but that data isn’t shared with me.

I don’t collect, store, or sell your personal information.
If you reach out via email, your message stays between us.
